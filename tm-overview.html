Traditional Marketing Overview

Explore Our Marketing Strategies

Print Marketing

Advertising through newspapers, magazines, brochures, and flyers to reach a targeted audience.

Broadcast Marketing

Promoting brands via television and radio commercials to attract a wide audience.

Outdoor Advertising

Billboards, posters, transit ads, and banners placed in high-traffic areas for visibility.

Direct Mail Marketing

Sending promotional materials like catalogs, postcards, and letters directly to consumers.

Telemarketing

Using phone calls to promote products, conduct surveys, or generate leads.

Event Marketing

Sponsoring or hosting events, trade shows, and exhibitions to interact directly with potential customers.

Word-of-Mouth Marketing

Encouraging satisfied customers to recommend products or services to others, boosting credibility.

Referral Marketing

Offering incentives for existing customers to refer new customers to a brand or business.

Face-to-Face Sales

In-person selling through sales representatives or door-to-door marketing to build personal connections.

Retail Marketing

Using in-store promotions, product displays, and branding efforts to attract customers at the point of sale.
